#Contributing to Legacy OpenStudio

We'd love for you to contribute to Legacy OpenStudio to make it better than it is today! Here are the guidelines we'd like you to follow:

 - [Question or Problem?](#question)
 - [Issues and Bugs](#issue)
 - [Feature Requests](#feature)
 - [Code Submission](#submit)
 
## <a name="question"></a> Got a Question or Problem?

Please direct questions about how to use OpenStudio to [unmethours](https://unmethours.com).  This is a community moderated resource and will provide the fastest answers to your questions.

## <a name="issue"></a> Found an Issue?
If you find a bug, you can help us by submitting an issue to our [issues page](https://github.com/NREL/legacy-openstudio/issues). Before submitting your issue please search existing issues to see if your bug has already been reported.  If reporting a new issue, please be as detailed as you can be in your report to help developers reproduce your issue so they can fix the problem.  For example, report the platform that you are on, the version of OpenStudio you are using, etc.
